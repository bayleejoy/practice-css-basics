What's Happening this Spring?

Spring time in Blacksburg is always filled with activities, but what exactly is going on? Below you'll find a quick guide on navigating the Blacksburg Spring Season which includes graduation, outdoor activities, the best drinking spots, etc.

Graduation Time

Graduation is a huge component of the Spring in Blacksburg. It's the only thing that seniors are focused on during the semester--besides finding a job.

Fun in the Sun

When there's free time outside of all the stress of closing out the year strong, you'll find most students at the local bars finding relaxation with friends. The most popular spot during the warm weather is Sharkey's due to its outside seating.

Ghost Attack Trends

Before we go anywhere, let's get this one thing straight: Ghosts are evil. Why? Because we said so. Specifically, we have decided that ghosts aren't human, but they're (at least somewhat) intelligent, so they must be evil. Don't question us, we're totally experts on this completely unexplored and unexperimented field! We made our assumptions long before we ever saw any ghosts, and once ghosts did start popping up, we immediately began biasing our observations in accordance to our assumptions, so we are always right. That's definitely how science works.

So far, what we see is that we are very bad at identifying ghosts, which is probably due to the fact that most of them move very quickly and are difficult to pin down, especially when they're fighting.

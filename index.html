More of My Rambling

African Grey Parrots are wonderfully intelligent birds that I am very fond of. I have one of my own, named Roxie, who is about to turn 12 in June, but she is not the one in the picture. The one you see here actually lives at the Knoxville Zoo in Tennessee and is named Einstein. Parrots make very rewarding, life-long companions. I'm not kidding, some species can live up to 80 years. An African Grey's cognitive abilites are comparable to a 3-year-old human child's, while their emotional capacity is equivalent to a 7-year-old's.
